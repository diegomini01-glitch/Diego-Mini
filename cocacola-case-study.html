Next-Gen Fan Engagement

Coca-Cola × Esports

Role Team lead · Concept author
Context IE Business School · MSMC
Brief Integrated Marketing Communication
Team 5 students · 2025

Coca-Cola wordmarks and brand visuals are not displayed on this public page. This was an academic team project; no engagement with Coca-Cola took place. The full deck is available on request under NDA.

The challenge

A legacy FMCG brand needed an authentic way into the esports ecosystem, beyond logo placement. The target audience (Gen Z gamers) is highly resistant to traditional advertising and brand intrusion into gameplay. Sponsorship for the sake of sponsorship doesn't work here; the brand has to earn its place inside the experience.

The insight

Gamers don't fear losing; they fear leaving 'the zone.' Their biggest enemy isn't a competitor; it's the energy crash mid-session. A drink brand has a credible role to play here, not as a sponsor, but as a performance system.

The concept: The Recharge Radar

An AI-powered gaming overlay that detects player fatigue through input patterns and webcam micro-behavior, then prompts a real-world recharge moment. Multi-platform delivery: downloadable overlay, OBS/Twitch extension, and API for games and Discord, meeting the audience where they already play. Brand integration happens at the fatigue-detection moment, with a real-time prompt and a gamified leaderboard tracking longest 'recharge streaks.'

Phase 1: The Recharge Hub (Tease)

An invite-only Discord server seeded into existing gaming communities. A custom bot, @Protocol_Recharge, drops cryptic invites: 'Recharge Protocol: Initiated.' FOMO and gated-access mechanics drive early hype before the brand reveal.

Phase 2: The Reveal

Following the Discord tease, Phase 2 transitions the audience from cryptic anticipation to brand reveal through gaming-influencer partnerships and a curated unboxing PR experience. Top-tier streamers receive a 'Recharge Boost' kit and stream their first encounter with the AI overlay live. Visual mockups from this phase are not shown on this public page to respect Coca-Cola's brand intellectual property. Full visuals are available in the deck on request.

Phase 3: Esports World Cup Popup (Activation)

The live-event extension: an on-site 'Energy Zone' at the Esports World Cup with a reaction-test mini-game, a digital leaderboard, and product sampling tied to the fatigue narrative. The full activation includes the venue concept, mini-game mechanics, and sampling strategy, available in the deck on request.

Research foundation

The Discord-first activation was grounded in consumer research:

  • 70%+ of Discord users identify as gamers
  • Brand-activated Discord servers (Chipotle, Riot Games) show 25–30% higher participation in challenges
  • Joining a brand Discord lifts purchase intent by 25%
  • Gated, invite-only access produces the strongest response

The activation map followed where the audience already lives, with measured purchase-intent uplift from community-based brand experiences.

What this work demonstrates

  • Translating an audience insight into a product-led brand activation, not a campaign
  • Designing AI-driven brand utility: a tool the audience uses rather than an ad they ignore
  • Channel and community design (Discord, Twitch, OBS extensions) for gaming audiences
  • Research-led activation grounded in deterministic and probabilistic consumer data
  • Multi-phase integrated marketing communication structure (tease, reveal, activation)
